## Full Description (4000 chars max)
Sage is your calm, always-on wellness companion. It combines AI-powered chat, mood tracking, guided journaling, daily habit building, breathing exercises, personalized wellness plans, and holistic wellness courses — all in one private, ad-free app.

Talk to Sage like a thoughtful friend. It listens, reflects, and offers gentle guidance — whether you need grounding, a new perspective, or just someone to think out loud with. Sage is not a therapist, but it's always here.

WHAT'S INSIDE:

Chat — Have a warm, private conversation with Sage. It remembers your context within each conversation, asks thoughtful follow-up questions, and suggests practical techniques when relevant.

Mood Tracking — One tap each day. Pick how you're feeling and watch your 7-day pattern emerge. No pressure, no streaks to break.

Journal — Write freely with AI-generated prompts tailored to your mood. Your entries stay on your device and are never shared.

Habits — Track five foundational daily habits: breathing practice, hydration, gratitude, movement, and sleep wind-down. See your streaks build naturally.

Breathing — A guided 4-4-6 breathing exercise that activates your parasympathetic nervous system. Breathe in for 4, hold for 4, exhale for 6. Calms the body fast.

Wellness Planner — Select your focus areas (meditation, fitness, nutrition, sleep, or habit building) and get a personalized 7-day wellness plan generated by AI, tuned to your goals.

Courses — Browse holistic wellness courses covering mindfulness, bodyweight fitness, nutrition and gut health, sleep optimization, and habit transformation. Free previews available.

PRIVACY FIRST:
- All data stored locally on your device
- No account required
- No ads, no tracking, no analytics
- No data collection of any kind
- Chat messages are processed in real time and never stored on servers

Sage is built for people who want a gentle, private space to check in with themselves every day — without the complexity of a full mental health platform or the noise of social features.

Download Sage and start your wellness journey today.
