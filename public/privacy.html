Privacy Policy

Effective date: April 16, 2026

Sage Wellness ("Sage", "we", "our", or "the app") is a personal wellness companion. Your privacy matters to us. This policy explains what data the app handles and how.

Data We Store

All personal data you enter — your name, mood entries, journal entries, habit logs, chat messages, and wellness plans — is stored locally on your device using your browser's localStorage. This data never leaves your device and is never transmitted to our servers or any third party.

AI Chat and Planner

When you use the Chat or Plan features, your messages are sent to an AI service (Anthropic) through our secure server to generate responses. We do not log, store, or retain the content of these messages on our servers. The messages are forwarded to the AI provider in real time and discarded immediately after a response is returned.

Data We Do Not Collect

We do not collect, store, or process any of the following: personal identification information, email addresses, location data, device identifiers, usage analytics, advertising IDs, cookies, or any form of tracking data.

No Accounts

Sage does not require you to create an account. There is no login, no registration, and no authentication. You start using the app immediately.

No Ads or Tracking

Sage contains no advertisements, no analytics trackers, no third-party SDKs, and no social media integrations. We do not sell, share, or monetize your data in any way.

Third-Party Services

The only third-party service used is Anthropic's API for AI-powered chat and wellness plan generation. Messages sent to this service are subject to Anthropic's Privacy Policy. No other third-party services receive your data.

Data Deletion

Since all data is stored locally on your device, you have full control. You can clear your data at any time by clearing your browser's localStorage or using the "Clear conversation" option within the app. Uninstalling the app removes all stored data.

Children's Privacy

Sage is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect any information from children.

Changes to This Policy

If we update this policy, the revised version will be posted at this URL with an updated effective date.
